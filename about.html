About Gupta Generators

Based structurally out of Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, Gupta Generators has grown to become an engineering leader in providing heavy-duty, state-of-the-art power backup solutions.

We specialized meticulously in industrial silent acoustic DG sets matching high environmental safety guidelines. With deployment models spanning from reliable infrastructure backups to rapid disaster assistance power setups, our team maintains full accountability from transportation to refueling and periodic onsite maintenance metrics.
